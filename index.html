* Perfection *

Some of us

love to chase

butterflies


Some have

milestones

to run after

* Perfection *

But there's one other

curious kind

that'd rather have perfection


For him,

Seas aren't

deep enough

* Perfection *

Mountains

challenge him not

and flowers lack color


He is looking

for something

breath-taking

* Perfection *

Something far

in lands unknown

and seas unexplored


He is restless

and this world,

only shackles

* Perfection *

To go there

he must

break free


Taking off

he looks back

one last time

* Perfection *

And right there

his eyes finally see

the extraordinary


the flowers

the sunset

and people and love

* Perfection *

His breath is gone,

his heart pounds,

and the winds whisper,


If this is not

perfection,

then what is?
